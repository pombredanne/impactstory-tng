About the data

Data sources

  • Altmetric supplies most of our data for tracking the online impact of publications.
  • BASE helps us find free fulltext for articles.
  • Mendeley gives us information about how articles are saved on their reference manager platform.
  • CrossRef helps us find metadata for articles that have DOIs.
  • ORCID provides researcher identity management services that form the backbone of our profile system.
  • Twitter supplies a convenient identity provider for signin (also, more Twitter analytics are on the roadmap).
